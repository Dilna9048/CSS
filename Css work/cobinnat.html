Fun Facts


Rabbits are small, furry mammals known for their long ears, short fluffy tails, and powerful hind legs used for hopping. They are herbivores, primarily eating grasses, hay, and vegetables. Rabbits are social animals, often living in groups, and are popular pets due to their gentle nature.
